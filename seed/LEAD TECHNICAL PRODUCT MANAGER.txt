Your Mission
 
We are hiring a Lead Technical Product Manager (TPM) to own end-to-end delivery of AI-powered solutions for our clients. As the single point of accountability for each engagement, you will translate ambitious product visions into shipped software — leading cross-functional teams of engineers, data and ML specialists, QA, and AI agents through a structured delivery lifecycle. 
From initial scoping and solution design through build, validation, and handoff, you will be the primary client contact and the delivery team’s north star. This is a principal-level role for someone who thrives at the intersection of technical depth, Product/Program governance, and client partnership
 
 
 
What You’ll Do
 
Lead End-to-End Delivery
Own the full delivery lifecycle: scoping, solution design, build, validation, and handoff across engagements ranging from rapid prototypes to production-grade enterprise solutions. 
Frame the problem, define what “done” looks like, assemble the right team, and drive execution through structured quality gates. 
Ensure live, deployed applications with CI/CD, cloud infrastructure, and AI tooling are configured and production-ready. 
Run retrospectives and extract reusable patterns from every engagement to strengthen future delivery. 
Own the Client Relationship 
Serve as the primary client contact across all engagements — from kick-off workshops through to final handoff. 
Run weekly check-ins, sprint reviews, steering committees, and structured demos using standardised delivery rituals. 
Manage stakeholder expectations, escalations, and feedback loops; drive client satisfaction through proactive communication and transparency. 
Build & Govern High-Performing Teams 
Assess engagement complexity and staff accordingly — from lean teams for prototypes to full cross-functional squads for enterprise-grade work. 
Manage resource allocation across phases, establishing clear RACI accountability for every workstream. 
Maintain delivery cadence: daily stand-ups, sprint planning, risk reviews, and retrospectives scaled to the engagement. 
Own the Product Lifecycle 
Own the Product Requirements Document (PRD) lifecycle: author, iterate, gain sign-off, manage change, and verify acceptance criteria. 
Drive engagement graduation (prototype → MVP → enterprise) on the same codebase with no rebuilds, ensuring solutions mature as client needs evolve. 
Manage scope changes and trade-off decisions, keeping delivery aligned to the agreed product vision and acceptance criteria. 
Drive Technical Quality 
Collaborate with engineers on Architecture Decision Records, technical design documents, and API specifications. 
Enforce quality gates across the delivery lifecycle — coordinating security scans, performance testing, and structured UAT. 
Oversee AI agent configuration and orchestration across the delivery team — including code-generation assistants, automated testing, and CI/CD agents. 
Measure & Report Business Value 
Track value metrics: time to delivery, technical velocity, quality scores, reuse rates, client satisfaction, and business impact. 
Deliver regular value reporting to stakeholders — from delivery snapshots to monthly business reviews and ROI analysis. 
Champion reusable asset development, ensuring every engagement contributes patterns and components back to the platform. 
 
What You Bring
 
8+ years of experience in technical programme management, delivery leadership, or technical product management in a consulting, agency, or technology environment. 
Proven track record delivering AI/ML or data-intensive products end-to-end, from concept through production launch. 
Strong understanding of modern software delivery: CI/CD, cloud infrastructure (AWS / GCP / Azure), microservices, and API-first architecture. 
Experience leading cross-functional teams (engineering, data, QA, security) through structured delivery frameworks with defined quality gates. 
Demonstrated ability to own client relationships at senior stakeholder level — including scoping, demos, UAT, and handoffs. 
Excellent communication skills: you can write a concise problem brief for a prototype and a comprehensive PRD with non-functional requirements for an enterprise engagement. 
Comfortable working with AI-assisted development tools and agentic workflows (e.g., LLM-based code generation, automated testing, AI-driven CI/CD). 
 
Nice to Have 
 
Hands-on experience with AI capability modules such as conversational AI, retrieval-augmented generation (RAG), or Text-to-SQL. 
Background in platform thinking — extracting reusable delivery patterns from project work and contributing them to a shared platform. 
Familiarity with code quality and security tooling (e.g., SonarQube, SAST/DAST) and compliance frameworks for regulated industries. 
Experience transitioning delivery engagements into managed support and maintenance models. 
Track record mentoring or managing a team of TPMs or delivery leads.
