# Job 3: AI Engineer — Enterprise SaaS (PLACEHOLDER)

## About the role
An enterprise SaaS company is hiring an AI Engineer to build LLM-powered features
into an established B2B product used by large organisations.

## Responsibilities
- Build LLM features: summarisation, semantic search, and assistant workflows.
- Own evaluation: offline eval sets, regression testing of prompts, quality metrics.
- Fine-tune retrieval quality: chunking strategies, hybrid search, reranking.
- Collaborate with product and design on AI UX patterns.

## Requirements
- Production experience shipping LLM-backed features.
- Python and/or TypeScript proficiency.
- Experience with vector databases and embedding models.
- Familiarity with eval frameworks and prompt regression testing.
- Understanding of AI safety, grounding, and hallucination mitigation.
