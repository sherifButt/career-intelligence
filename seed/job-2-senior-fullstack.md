# Job 2: Senior Full-Stack Engineer — Fintech Scale-up (PLACEHOLDER)

## About the role
A fintech scale-up seeks a senior full-stack engineer to own features across the stack
on a payments platform serving thousands of merchants.

## Responsibilities
- Ship features across React frontend and Node.js/Go backend services.
- Design and evolve PostgreSQL schemas under high transaction volume.
- Improve observability, alerting, and incident response.
- Mentor mid-level engineers and review code.

## Requirements
- 5+ years full-stack experience; strong React and Node.js.
- Deep PostgreSQL knowledge: indexing, query planning, migrations at scale.
- Experience with event-driven architectures (Kafka or similar).
- Kubernetes and infrastructure-as-code (Terraform) experience.
- Financial services or payments domain experience is a plus.
